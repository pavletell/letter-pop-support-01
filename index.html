LetterPop Support

Last updated: May 12, 2026

LetterPop is a word-building game for iPhone and iPad. Pop floating letter balls in the right order to spell words, unlock animal friends, and play in 10 languages. This page answers common questions — if you don't find what you need, email devpavletell@gmail.com.

Getting started

How do I play?

A target word appears at the top of the screen. Tap the letter balls in order, from left to right, to spell it. Each correct letter scores points, and completing the word triggers a celebration. The wrong letter shakes and dings — no penalty, just try again.

How does scoring work?

Each letter gives you 5 base points multiplied by your current multiplier. The multiplier doubles each time you reach a milestone, so longer streaks earn much more.

What are the animal characters?

The little animal in the top-left corner is your companion. You start with the Otter and unlock more animals (each with their own peek animation) by reaching score milestones. Animals stay unlocked even if you reset your score.

Languages & word lists

Which languages are supported?

English, Italian, French, German, Russian, Ukrainian, Korean, Chinese, Japanese, and Arabic. You can switch languages anytime from Settings.

Can I use my own word list?

Yes. LetterPop accepts plain text files (.txt) or its own .lpwords format — one word per line. To import:

  • Save the file to the Files app, iCloud Drive, or AirDrop it to your device.
  • Open the file and choose "Open in LetterPop", or
  • Copy the file into LetterPop's folder via Finder (iPhone/iPad → Files → LetterPop).
  • Open Settings inside the app and select the new list.

Files should be UTF-8, UTF-16, or ASCII. If letters look garbled, re-save the file as UTF-8 and import again.

How do I change word length?

Open Settings and pick a maximum word length from 3 to 7 letters. Shorter words make for a faster, easier game; longer words are more challenging.

How do I delete a custom word list?

Open Settings, find your imported list, and tap the delete control next to it. Built-in languages can't be removed.

Sound & speech

Why doesn't the app speak words on the first try?

LetterPop uses your device's built-in voices. The first time a word is spoken, iOS may need a moment to load the voice. To make speech instant, open Settings inside LetterPop and tap "Pre-cache audio" — this downloads audio for every word in the current list. The settings stay locked while caching runs; just let it finish.

The voice for my language sounds robotic or is missing.

Voice quality is controlled by iOS. To install higher-quality voices, go to iOS Settings → Accessibility → Spoken Content → Voices, pick your language, and download an enhanced or premium voice. Restart LetterPop and the new voice will be used.

In-app purchases

Are there ads or required purchases?

No. LetterPop has no ads, no subscriptions, and no paywalled features. Everything in the game is available for free.

What does "Support Development" do?

It's a fully optional tip jar. You can leave a one-time tip ($0.99, $1.99, or $4.99) to help fund development. It doesn't unlock anything — it's just a thank-you.

How do I restore a previous purchase?

Open the "Support Development" screen and tap Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in to the same Apple ID you used originally.

I want a refund.

Refunds for App Store purchases are handled by Apple, not by the developer. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com, sign in with your Apple ID, find the LetterPop transaction, and submit a refund request.

Data, reset, and privacy

Does LetterPop collect any data?

No. LetterPop does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Scores, unlocked animals, and your word-list preferences are saved only on your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I reset my score?

Open Settings and tap Reset Game. This clears your score but keeps the animals you've unlocked.

How do I erase everything?

Open Settings and tap Delete App Data. This removes all scores, unlocked animals, and imported word lists — the app returns to its first-launch state. Deleting the app from your home screen also removes all local data.

Devices & requirements

Which devices does LetterPop run on?

iPhone and iPad running iOS / iPadOS 18.6 or later. iPhone uses portrait orientation; iPad supports portrait and landscape.

Does the game sync across devices?

Not currently — progress is stored locally on each device.

Troubleshooting

The app froze or letters stopped responding.

Swipe up to close LetterPop and reopen it. If it happens repeatedly, restart your device. If the issue persists, please email us with your device model and iOS version.

An imported word list shows no words.

Most likely the file's text encoding isn't supported. Open the file in a plain text editor (TextEdit on macOS, for example), use Format → Make Plain Text, save as UTF-8, and import again.

Still need help?
Email devpavletell@gmail.com. Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the problem (a screenshot helps a lot).
